Two different topics designed to exist on the same page. I used different visual systems to convey their meaning and make them stand apart from each other. Running on the top is 1960s Advertising, set in Century Expanded, a typeface used commonly in the imagery.

Along the bottom is 1960s Psychedelic Culture, set in Futura, another typeface from the period chosen to contrast the funky, psychedelic posters.
